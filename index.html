Our Story

Hello my Name is Beatrice Cooke,

I am the owner and designer for Katy Rose Floral. We are a floral event company based in Katy, Texas established in June of 2019. We are family owned and operated. I have been in the floral, wedding and event business for over 10 years as a designer.

Our passion, experience and professionalism are unveiled in our floral products and services. We strive to bring creativity, honesty and attention to detail to each and every event. It gives me great pleasure to create a vision and touch someone's life at such a meaningful time in their life.

Katy Rose Floral proudly serves the Katy Texas area for Weddings, Quinceaneras, Private and Corporate Events. We offer a customized selection of Bridal Bouquets, Ceremony Flowers, including Garlands and Centerpieces using a quality selection of flowers. It would be my pleasure to meet with you for a consultation to unveil your dream event, please reach out to us today.
